PERSONAL LIFE

Kimberly Noel Kardashian born October 21,
1980 is an American socialite, model, media
personality, and businesswoman. (wikipedia)

She first gained media attention as a friend
and stylist of Paris Hilton. Later that year, she
and her family began to appear in the E!
reality television series Keeping Up with the
Kardashians 2007–2021. Its success led to
the formation of the spin-off series. (wikipedia)

Her relationship with rapper Kanye West has
also received significant media coverage;
they married in 2014 and have four children
together. (wikipedia)

Time magazine included Kardashian on their
list of 2015's 100 most influential people.
Both critics and admirers have described her
as exemplifying the notion of being famous
for being famous. (wikipedia)

BRANDS

Social media queen Kim Kardashian has
parlayed reality TV into an actual fortune,
selling a mobile game, cosmetics and, now, shapewear. (crunchbase)

KKW Beauty is a digitally native beauty
brand that offers skincare products such as
lipsticks, eyeliner, moisturizers, and
cleansers. The company was founded in
2017 by the American model Kim
Kardashian West. (Forbes)

In June 2021, Kardashian revealed that her
brand Skims would provide undergarments,
loungewear, and pajamas, and other
clothing items with American flags and the
Olympics rings with a Team USA branding
printed on them to the Team USA at the
2020 Summer Olympics and Paralympics.
In October 2021, it was announced that
luxury fashion house Fendi would do a
capsule collection with Skims. (wikipedia)

SLIVING SISTERS

Kim’s career began as stylist to close friend Paris
Hilton. The two were inseparable, often wearing
coordinated looks. Kim made regular appearances
on Paris and Nicole Richie’s iconic reality show, The
Simple Life, and travelled with the former to events
worldwide. After nights of partying in Sydney, the
two spent their hangover days shopping in
matching outfits: metallic bathing suits under white
tunics and sarongs, comfy flats, dark sunglasses,
and, of course, colossal LV bags swinging on their
arms. (i-D)

BALMAIN BABE

The fashion house Balmain became synonymous
with the Kardashian-Jenners, namely Kim,
Kendalland Kylie. The girls were often seen
sporting the house’s signature beaded gowns, with
their military styles and Renaissance inspirations,
partly due to their close personal friendship with
creative director Olivier Rousteing. Here, Kim wears
a heavily-beaded pale blue Balmain dress in Paris,
just days before she was set to marry Kanye West. (i-D)
