and his sketch book







I have been creating and decorating since a young age. I like to take inspiration from things I see and adapt them to my point of view. I mix mediums like magazines, drawings, and collages to build my vision









tokyo pinup


The pinup cutouts are from a book I got downtown. It was filled with vintage advertisements from the 1950s. The girl smoking was a drawing I found on @kencitydraws Instagram page.









icon angel


The angel and Holder cutouts are from a book I got at school called the "Art Book". The cutouts of Winnie the Pooh and Bugs Bunny are from The Little Golden Books.










midnight fairies


The Cinderella cutouts and backgrounds are from The Little Golden Books. The array of angels on the side is drawn and pulled inspiration from the @nephrosoupp Instagram page.









star gazing


The whole collage is a mishmash of inspiration taken from Juliana Horner. I used different mediums like acrylic paint, post-it notes, letter stickers and felts.
