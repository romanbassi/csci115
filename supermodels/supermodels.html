established herself amongst the most recognisable and

KATE MOSS

Katherine Ann Moss born 16 January 1974, is an
British model and businesswoman. (wikipedia)

Arriving at the end of the supermodel era, Moss rose
to fame in the early 1990s as part of the heroin chic
fashion trend. (wikipedia)

Her collaborations with Calvin Klein brought her to
fashion icon status. She is known for
her waifish figure, and role in
size zero fashion. (wikipedia)

CINDY CRAWFORD

Cynthia Ann Crawford born February 20, 1966, is an
American fashion model and television personality. (britannica)

She first gained fame in the 1980s and was among
the first supermodels. In 1986 Crawford relocated to New York City
to pursue a professional modeling career. Touted as Baby Gia
in reference to her resemblance to the late top fashion model
Gia Carangi, Crawford immediately secured work.(britannica)

In 1995 Crawford appeared in a major advertising campaign for
PepsiCo, Inc., and was named the top-earning model in the
world by the U.S. publishing and media company Forbes. (britannica)

YASMEEN GHAURI

Yasmeen Ghauri born March 23, 1971, is an
Canadian former model. (wikipedia)

Ghauri actively pursued a modeling career, debuting in
Milan and Paris before moving to New
York City in 1990. Here, she was noticed
by fashion critics and labels alike.
(wikipedia)

The New York Times described her runway walk as a ball-bearing
swivel of her hips. Her appearance prompted part of what
is now termed in fashion the Canadian invasion.(wikipedia)

BELLA HADID

Isabella Khair Hadid born October 9, 1996, is an
American model. (wikipedia)

In 2016, she was voted Model of the Year by industry
professionals for Models.com. Over the span
of four years, Hadid has made twenty-seven
appearances on international Vogue magazine covers.(wikipedia)

In January 2016, Hadid made her Chanel Couture debut during Paris
Haute Couture Spring/Summer Fashion Week. She also
starred in Marc Jacobs' campaign, "My America". In February, she walked
for Fenty x Puma at New York Fashion Week.(wikipedia)
