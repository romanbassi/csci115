HOT PINK

The color has been seen on many spring/summer
2022 runways from Balenciaga to Dior to Valentino.

The color has been popularized by the Balenciaga's Demna Gvasalia and
Kim Kardashian's collab. She was first seen hosting the late-night comedy
show Saturday Night Live on October 9, 2021, sporting the bright pink color.

Kim also jumped on the pink color way and decided to do a full pink
collection called "Valentine's Day Capsule Collection" for her brand Skims.

Valentino also did a whole collection in fall/winter 2022-23
Pink PP Collection. "Creative Director Pierpaolo Piccioli conceives the
new Valentino collection following the experimental urge of a radical gesture.
Stripping the palette down to a single hue, relentlessly, he does more with
less, maximizing expressive possibilities in the apparent lack of possibilities." (Valentino)

MINISKIRTS

The early 2000's aesthetic has been making its way back into fashion.
Including exposed whale tale thongs, trucker hats, and low rise jeans.
But the thing that has popped out full force is miniskirts.

"Miu Miu first presented the micro-mini skirt during its spring 2022
fashion show last October. After the skirt was seen on nearly every
red carpet, designer Miuccia Prada revamped the daring style for her
fall 2022 collection, which just hit the Paris fashion week catwalk." (The New York Post)

Thee Miu Miu outfit has been taking over your social media
feeds lately. The Miu Miu set featuring a cropped sweatshirt
and a low-waisted miniskirt, has been worn by
literally everybody in the it girl circuit

Thanks to brands like Miu Miu and Diesel, the mini
skirt is one of the defining trends of 2022.

PLATFORM PUMPS

The early 1990's aesthetic has been here to stay in fashion.
Including flannels, baggy jeans, and leather jackets.
But the thing that is giving us our Spice World life is platforms.

"This trend kicked off with Versace showing a selection of colorful satin
and leather versions on the runway at their Fall/Winter 2021 show.
The shoes were immediately styled in real life by Dua Lipa
and Ariana Grande, and then other brands like
Valentino followed suit with similar options." (Marie Claire)

Celebrities like Ariana Grande, Dua Lipa, Nicola Peltz-Beckham, and
more have all been spotted in ultra-daring platform pumps.
You'll see that platform-style sandals were everywhere
this year. Just about every celebrity has been sporting them recently.
The timeless silhouette is a lot comfier than your usual strappy pair. (Marie Claire)

Versace platform pumps are normally made from satin (and are very, very expensive),
but the market is chock-full of similar styles to shop right now.
You have my explicit permission to live out your 2000s
fashion fantasies this spring and summer. (Marie Claire)
